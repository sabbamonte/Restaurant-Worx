About

If you've worked in the NYC hospitality industry, you know how hard it is to find the right restaurant that pays well, is consistent and has a good environment. There may be websites to look up employee reviews but it's near impossible to find in depth info specifically for the hospitality industry.

Most of the time you don't even know how much you can earn until your first day of work. You start and are finally able to talk to a co-worker only to find that the pay is not what you expected or hoped to be. That sucks. Now you've wasted time you could have spent searching for the right job.

This is where ResWorx comes in.



Search

Search restaurants for employee reviews, see how much you could earn and if it's worth it to you.

Review

Review your own work place to help others.

Security

Always anonymous and no personal information is shared with anybody.
